## How to Calculate Your Norwegian Mortgage (/boliglan)
Total time: PT5M

1. **Enter your loan amount** — Input total mortgage amount (property price minus equity). Need at least 15% equity for primary residence.
2. **Set the interest rate** — Enter nominal interest rate. 2026 typical range: 4.5%-6.0%.
3. **Choose the loan term** — Select repayment period (max 30 years). Shorter = higher payments but less total interest.
4. **Compare annuity vs serial loan** — Review monthly payments, total interest cost, remaining balance for both types.
5. **Run the stress test** — Check if you can afford payments at rate +3pp (min 7%). Required under Utlaansforskriften.
6. **Review total costs** — Check dokumentavgift (2.5%), tinglysingsgebyr, effective interest rate, and 22% tax deduction on interest.

## How to Create a Personal Budget in Norway (/personlig)
Total time: PT15M

1. **List all income sources** — Enter monthly net income from all sources (salary, freelance, rental, student loans, NAV benefits).
2. **Enter fixed expenses** — Rent/mortgage, insurance, loan repayments, fixed subscriptions.
3. **Add variable necessities** — Groceries (3,000-5,000 kr/person), electricity, transport, phone/internet.
4. **Track discretionary spending** — Dining out, entertainment, clothing, hobbies, streaming, gym.
5. **Review your budget balance** — Income minus expenses. Aim for 10-20% savings rate.
6. **Export and track** — Download as CSV. Review quarterly. Set up automatic savings transfers.
